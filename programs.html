Industry Talk 3

Topic:

Information Systems Behind Power BI

Date:

14 January 2026

Reflection:

Throughout the talk, the speaker introduced the background of PPG company and she explained how Power BI operates using a dessert-making analogy. This made the sharing session even more interactive and easy to understand. I realized that Power BI is more than just a software because it can connect with many data sources and it has three main components (desktop, service and mobile). Another important point that the speaker addressed was the demonstration of the appropriate settings of the VPN in order to ensure that the right people access the right data. All in all, this talk reminded me the importance of continuous learning. Although certain courses are tough, I realized that I need to always enhance my knowledge, as well as my communication skills. I understood that I must make full use of the knowledge provided by UTM and take every opportunity to upskill myself.

ASEAN Quantum Summit 2025

Date:

11 December 2025

Reflection:

This event included topics like quantum computing and quantum communication well as education and the environment that helps new businesses grow. From this talk, I knew that Malaysia wants to be a leader in this field of science in the region. Prof. Dr. Masahiro Kitagawa from Japan talked about what Japan's doing with quantum science. He said it can be used in lots of ways especially when it comes to living things. At the same time, Mr. Ling Keok Tong from Singapore also shared his ideas on what Singapore's planning to do with quantum science. There was also an exhibition booth visit where we can look at the research that is being done and see the technology that is being developed. This really helped us to understand them better. Overall, this event inspired interest in quantum technology among university students and future collaborations.
